  • About Course

    This Online Course aims to expose students to the higher level of problem solving in mathematics. Specially designed for 8th class kids who wish to appear in PRE RMO and other math Olympiads from 9th Class onwards. The Course aims to develop super strong basics in mathematics which will help the child ease through the journey of Olympiads and other competitive exams. Alongside mathematics lectures of Mental Ability will also be conducted to parallel give children exposure for NTSE examination which is conducted in Class X.
