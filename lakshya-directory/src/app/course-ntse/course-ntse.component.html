Explore Mental Ability for NTSE

  • About Course

    This Online Course aims to expose students to the Mental Ability problems asked in NTSE (National Talent Search Exam). Specially designed for 9th and 10th class kids who wish to prepare for NTSE, IMO and future competitive examinations. The Course aims to develop super strong basics of Mental Ability and thinking skills which will help the child ease through the journey of competitive exams.
